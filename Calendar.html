Sri Lanka National Events Calendar

Vesak

Vesak is the most significant Buddhist festival in Sri Lanka, commemorating the birth, enlightenment, and death of Lord Buddha. The day is celebrated with vibrant decorations, processions, and religious rituals.

Religious Festival

Date: May (Full Moon Day)

Esala Perahera (Kandy)

The Esala Perahera is one of the grandest and most well-known cultural festivals in Sri Lanka, featuring a mesmerizing procession of traditional dancers, drummers, fire-breathers, and elephants carrying sacred relics.

Cultural Festival

Date: July/August

Independence Day

Independence Day marks the anniversary of Sri Lanka's independence from British colonial rule. The day is observed with flag hoisting, military parades, and various cultural events across the country.

National Day

Date: February 4th

Thai Pongal

A major Hindu festival celebrated by the Tamil community, Thai Pongal marks the harvest season and is a time to thank the Sun God for a bountiful crop. It is celebrated with special prayers, feasts, and the preparation of the traditional Pongal dish.

Religious Festival

Date: January 14th

Sinhala & Tamil New Year

This traditional New Year marks the end of the harvest season and is celebrated by both the Sinhala and Tamil communities. It includes traditional rituals, games, and special foods like "Kavum" (oil cakes) and "Kiribath" (milk rice).

Cultural Festival

Date: April 13th-14th

Date: May 18th

Christmas

Christmas is celebrated by Christians across Sri Lanka to honor the birth of Jesus Christ. It is a time for family gatherings, church services, and sharing goodwill. Streets, homes, and churches are decorated with lights and Christmas trees.

Religious Festival

Date: December 25th

Colombo Art Biennale

The Colombo Art Biennale is a premier international contemporary art exhibition held every two years. It brings together artists from across the globe to showcase their work in various artistic mediums.

Cultural Festival

Date: Varies (Biennial)

Republic Day

Republic Day marks the day Sri Lanka became a republic, officially ending the British monarchy. The day is commemorated with parades, speeches, and various celebrations across the country.

National Day

Date: May 22nd
